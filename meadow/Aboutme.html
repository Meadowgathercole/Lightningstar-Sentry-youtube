Why I like My little pony

When I was in 5th grade I was always making friends, but I had a hard time keeping them. My parents say its due to my outbursts and not being able to contorl my impulses. This is most likely due to my Autism, ADHD, and ODD. The day I found out about My little pony was during summer right before I started 6th grade. I was at my grandmothers house watching t.v. I was so bored so I just turned on the first show I saw which happened to be My little Pony Friendship is magic. Now when I first saw the name I was like "Omg I remember watching the older versions as a kid until they were discontinued." I started watching it expecting something lame... boy was I wrong. The very first episode I watched was Season one episode 2 "Friendship is Magic Part 2." At first I thought it was going to be a once in a blue moon when I watched it, but then everytime I went over to my grandmothers I watched it over and over again. The more I watched the more I learned about friendship qualities. Like Honesty is always the best policy, being a loyal friend is a perfect quality in a friend. Ever since I started watching I have been able to make friends and even keep them longer than I used to. For example, in 7th grade I made a friend that just happened to be interested in the same things as me. She liked My little Pony, art and everyonce in a while she loved playing video games.
